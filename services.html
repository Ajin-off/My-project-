Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you deliver to? +

We currently deliver within a 10-mile radius of our restaurant location. Delivery is available Tuesday through Sunday from 5pm to 10pm. A minimum order of $50 is required for delivery.

How far in advance should I book catering? +

We recommend booking catering services at least 3 weeks in advance for events under 50 people, and 6-8 weeks in advance for larger events. Last-minute bookings may be available depending on our schedule.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions? +

Yes, we can accommodate most dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and common allergies. Please inform us of any restrictions when making your reservation or placing your order.

What is your cancellation policy? +

For private dining and catering, cancellations made more than 7 days in advance receive a full refund. Between 7-3 days, we charge 50% of the estimated total. Within 72 hours, the full amount is non-refundable.
